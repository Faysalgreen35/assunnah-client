💻 How to Install

🤖 Android Phone (Chrome)

Step 1: Open Chrome and visit assunnah.com
Step 2: Tap the ⋮ (three dots) menu at the top right
Step 3: Select "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
Step 4: Tap "Install" in the popup
✅ Done! The app appears on your home screen.

🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari)

Step 1: Open Safari and visit assunnah.com
Step 2: Tap the Share button (⬆️) at the bottom
Step 3: Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
Step 4: Enter a name (e.g., "As-Sunnah") and tap "Add"
✅ Done! The app icon appears on your home screen.
Note: Works best on iOS 16.4 and newer

💻 Windows / Mac / Linux (Desktop)

Step 1: Visit assunnah.com in Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox
Step 2: Look for the Install icon in the address bar
Or: Click ⋮ menu → "Install app"
Step 3: Click "Install" in the popup
Step 4: Choose location (Desktop, Apps menu, etc.)
✅ Done! Desktop shortcut or app menu entry created.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the app the same as the website?

A: Yes! It's the exact same store. The app is just a faster, easier way to access it.

Q: Do I need an account?

A: Only to place orders and track deliveries. Browsing works without an account.

Q: Will it work on my old phone?

A: Android 5.0+, iPhone iOS 12.2+. Most devices are compatible. Very old phones may have issues.

Q: Can I uninstall the app?

A: Yes, anytime. Just like any other app:

  • Android: Long press the icon → "Uninstall"
  • iPhone: Long press the icon → "Remove App"
  • Desktop: Drag to trash or remove from apps menu

Q: What data does the app save?

A:

  • Your login session (you stay signed in)
  • Product images (for faster loading)
  • Previously visited pages (for offline access)
  • Cart and wishlist (on your account)
We NEVER save passwords or payment info on your device.

Q: Why won't the app install?

A: Try these steps:

  1. Use Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Close and reopen your browser
  4. Clear browser cache (Settings → Privacy)
  5. If still not working, email us

Q: How much storage does the app use?

A: Very little — about 5-20 MB initially. Cached images may take 50-100 MB depending on your browsing.

Q: Will the app work without internet?

A: Partially:

  • ✅ Browse cached products
  • ✅ View saved wishlist
  • ✅ Read order history
  • ❌ Can't place new orders
  • ❌ Can't see live prices

🆘 Troubleshooting

App Won't Install

  1. Update your browser to the latest version
  2. Clear browser cache: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
  3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox)
  4. Restart your device
  5. Try on WiFi (not mobile data)

App Loads Slowly

  1. Check your internet speed
  2. Close other apps running in background
  3. Restart your device
  4. Clear app cache in Settings

Can't See Latest Products

  1. Force close and reopen the app
  2. Swipe down to refresh the page
  3. Clear the app cache
  4. Reinstall the app if needed

Can't Checkout or Place Order

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Go back and retry
  3. Try in a web browser instead
  4. Contact us if issue persists

Lost My Cart or Wishlist

Don't worry! Your data is safe:

  1. Sign in with your account
  2. All saved items reappear
  3. Nothing is lost unless you delete your account
