## 💻 How to Install on Different Devices

### **📱 Android Phone (Chrome)**

**Step 1:** Open Chrome and visit **assunnah.com** (or copy the link from your browser)

**Step 2:** Tap the **⋮ (three dots)** menu at the top right

**Step 3:** Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**

**Step 4:** Tap **"Install"** in the popup

**✅ Done!** The app appears on your home screen.

---

### **🍎 iPhone / iPad (Safari)**

**Step 1:** Open Safari and visit **assunnah.com**

**Step 2:** Tap the **Share button** (looks like ⬆️) at the bottom

**Step 3:** Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**

**Step 4:** Enter a name (e.g., "As-Sunnah") and tap **"Add"**

**✅ Done!** The app icon appears on your home screen.

> **Note:** The app works best in iOS 16.4 and newer. You may get a prompt to add it to your dock too!

---

### **💻 Windows / Mac / Linux (Desktop)**

**Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari:**

**Step 1:** Visit **assunnah.com** in your browser

**Step 2:** Look for the **Install icon** in the address bar (looks like 📦 or a box)
- Some browsers: Click **⋮ menu** → "Install app"

**Step 3:** Click **"Install"** in the popup

**Step 4:** Choose location (Desktop, Apps menu, etc.)

**✅ Done!** Desktop shortcut or app menu entry created.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### **App Won't Install**

**Try:**
1. Update your browser to the latest version
2. Clear browser cache: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data
3. Close and reopen browser
4. Try again on WiFi (not mobile data)

**Still not working?**
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox)
- Restart your device
- Contact us: **support@assunnah.com**

---

### **App Loads Slowly**

**Fix:**
1. Check your internet speed
2. Close other apps running in background
3. Restart your device
4. Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → As-Sunnah → Storage → Clear Cache)

---

### **Can't See Latest Products**

**Fix:**
1. Force close and reopen the app
2. Swipe down to refresh (pull-to-refresh)
3. Clear the app cache
4. Reinstall the app

---

### **Can't Checkout or Place Order**

**Fix:**
1. Check your internet connection (need WiFi or mobile data)
2. Go back and retry
3. Try in a browser instead
4. Contact us if issue persists

---

### **Lost My Cart or Wishlist**

**Don't Worry!** Your data is safe:
- Sign in with your account
- All saved items reappear
- Nothing is lost unless you delete your account

---

### **App Keeps Closing**

**Try:**
1. Force close: Swipe the app away from recent apps
2. Clear app cache: Settings → Apps → As-Sunnah → Storage
3. Restart your phone
4. Reinstall the app
